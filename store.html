About Made with Corazón

The sacred heart has been a powerful symbol in Mexican and Chicano culture, representing divine love, compassion, and spiritual protection. "Made with Corazón" honors this tradition while bringing it into contemporary wearable art.

Each pin is a small meditation on faith, culture, and craft—combining traditional felt bead embroidery techniques with modern design sensibilities. These pieces are meant to be worn as talismans, carrying the sacred heart's protective energy while celebrating the beauty of handmade art.

The collection name, "Made with Corazón" (Made with Heart), reflects both the literal sacred heart imagery and the emotional investment in each handcrafted piece.
