About This Shop

Welcome to my handmade shop, where each piece is crafted with intention and care. As a multimedia artist, I create accessories that carry the same spirit and cultural significance as my larger works.

Every item is made by hand in small batches, ensuring quality and uniqueness. From felt embroidered pins to meaningful milagros keychains, each piece tells a story and connects to the broader themes of identity, heritage, and artistic expression.
