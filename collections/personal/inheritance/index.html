Inheritance

Medium: Digital Art Year: 2025 Dimensions: 24" x 30"

About The Piece

"Inheritance" is a deeply personal surreal self-portrait that explores the complex relationship between individual identity and ancestral legacy. The piece delves into the quiet weight of inherited wounds—both seen and unseen—that pass through generations like shadows cast by those who came before us.

Through digital manipulation and symbolic imagery, this work examines how sacred feminine power flows through bloodlines, carrying with it both strength and trauma. The surreal elements reflect the way ancestral memory lives within us, sometimes emerging in dreams, sometimes in the curve of our hands or the way we hold our grief.

Created during a period of deep introspection about family history and cultural identity, "Inheritance" serves as both a reckoning with the past and a meditation on what we choose to carry forward. It speaks to the Chicana experience of navigating between worlds, honoring ancestors while carving out space for healing and transformation.
