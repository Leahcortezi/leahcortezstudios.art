Inheritance

Medium: Digital Art Year: 2025 Dimensions: 12" x 16"

About The Piece

Inheritance confronts the complex terrain of cultural identity and generational trauma within the Chicana experience. Drawing inspiration from Frida Kahlo's unflinching self-examination, this digital composition presents a figure deliberately stripped of conventional cultural markers—not through rejection, but through the quiet violence of assimilation.

Surrounded by thorned native flora, the subject exists in suspended tension between survival and vulnerability. The work emerges from the liminal space of being a "no sabo" kid—raised between worlds, never fully belonging to either. Each visual element speaks to the inherited burden of erasure, the generational silence that shapes identity through absence rather than presence.

Through restrained symbolism and deliberate omissions, Inheritance poses an essential question: when erasure becomes legacy, how do we claim our right to belong?
