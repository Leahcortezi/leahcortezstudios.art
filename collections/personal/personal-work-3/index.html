Inner Landscape

Medium: Oil on canvas Year: 2023 Dimensions: 30" x 40"

About The Piece

This deeply personal work maps the terrain of introspection, using symbolic imagery and color to represent emotional states and psychological landscapes. The composition suggests both external and internal geography.

Created during a period of intense self-reflection, the piece explores themes of growth, transformation, and the complex topography of the human psyche. Each brushstroke represents a step deeper into understanding.
