Heaven on Fire

Medium: Mixed media

Dimensions: 8" × 10"

Year: 2021

About The Piece

This surreal landscape imagines paradise at the edge of collapse—where heaven blooms with chaos and spots of hell begin to leak through. Using colored pencil, watercolor, and ink, I layered bold, intuitive colors with smooth gradients and textured mark-making to harmonize disorder and space.

Each color was chosen by instinct—sometimes randomly, sometimes with purpose—to create structured randomness. The piece evokes a dreamlike world where beauty, fear, and transformation coexist.
