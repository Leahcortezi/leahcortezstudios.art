Cosmic Dance

Medium: Acrylic and gold leaf on canvas Year: 2023 Dimensions: 36" x 48"

About The Piece

This vibrant work celebrates the universal rhythms that connect all life, from the spinning of planets to the beating of hearts. The gold leaf elements catch and reflect light, creating a sense of movement and energy.

The composition suggests both microscopic and macroscopic views of existence, inviting viewers to consider their place in the vast dance of the cosmos. The interplay of organic and geometric forms speaks to the harmony underlying apparent chaos.
