Abuela's Altar

Medium: Charcoal Year: 2025 Dimensions: 18" x 24"

About The Piece

Rendered in charcoal and inspired by Churrigueresque Catholic altars, this still life uses deep contrast and texture to evoke memory and devotion. Built from my own objects, each element reflects how we hold on to my grandmother, Irma—through prayer, through tarot, through the broken things she left behind.

She passed down her faith with fear and responsibility.
All she ever wanted was to get into heaven.
This is my way of keeping her close—and hoping she made it.
