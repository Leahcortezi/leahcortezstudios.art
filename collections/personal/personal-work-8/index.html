Soft Undoing

Medium: Mixed media on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 32" x 24"

About The Piece

This deeply personal work explores the connection between past and present, weaving together symbols and memories that span generations. The layered composition reflects the complexity of cultural inheritance and identity.

Using a combination of traditional and contemporary techniques, the piece honors ancestral wisdom while asserting contemporary voice. Each element carries meaning, creating a visual narrative of continuity and change.
