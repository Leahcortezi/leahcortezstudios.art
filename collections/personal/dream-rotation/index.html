Dream Rotation

Medium: Digital Art (Adobe Illustrator) Year: 2025 Dimensions: 8.5" x 5.5" Type: Sticker Sheet

About The Piece

“Dream Rotation” is a personal sticker sheet designed as a heartfelt and humorous gift for a close friend, inspired by their tattoo style and love for whimsical imagery. Each character is drawn in a playful doodle style, with its own unique posture and puff of smoke—reflecting different moods and personalities with a touch of satire.

The composition forms a circular “rotation,” symbolizing the ongoing cycles of dreams, friendships, and creative flow. The addition of tiny stars scattered throughout the background evokes a dreamy, celestial atmosphere that ties each sticker into the larger theme.

This project blends digital illustration and personal storytelling. I used Adobe Illustrator to craft the designs and my Cricut to precisely cut each sticker, ensuring they could function both as standalone icons and a cohesive sheet. The result is a balance of humor, sentiment, and charm—a thoughtful handmade gift that captures the quirks of friendship through design.
