Anointed Gaze

Medium: Mixed media on wood panel Year: 2024 Dimensions: 24" x 24"

About The Piece

This contemplative work explores the mathematical principles underlying natural forms, using precise geometric patterns as a foundation for organic expression. The piece bridges the gap between analytical and intuitive thinking.

The square format reinforces the themes of balance and proportion, while the layered materials create depth and texture that invite close examination. The work suggests that beauty emerges from the intersection of order and spontaneity.
