Entre Mundos

Medium: Watercolor and ink on paper Year: 2023 Dimensions: 22" x 30"

About The Piece

This delicate work explores the boundary between the physical and spiritual worlds, using botanical forms as metaphors for growth, renewal, and transcendence. The translucent quality of watercolor creates an otherworldly atmosphere.

Inspired by dreams and meditation practices, the piece suggests a garden that exists beyond ordinary perception - a space where the soul can flourish and find peace. The flowing lines and organic forms invite contemplation.
