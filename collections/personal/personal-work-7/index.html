Transformation

Medium: Oil and charcoal on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 30" x 40"

About The Piece

This powerful work captures the essence of metamorphosis - both literal and metaphorical. Using dramatic contrasts of light and shadow, the piece explores themes of change, growth, and rebirth that are central to the human experience.

The interplay between oil paint's richness and charcoal's immediacy creates a dynamic tension that mirrors the often challenging but ultimately rewarding process of personal transformation. Each mark on the canvas represents a step in this ongoing journey.
