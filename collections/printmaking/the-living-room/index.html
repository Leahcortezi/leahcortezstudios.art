The Living Room

Medium: Reduction Linocut Print Year: 2025

Project Overview

The Living Room was born from the assignment to create a creature—but instead of designing a single being, I imagined an entire underwater alien landscape that functions as one living organism. Everything in this scene—the coral-like structures, bulbous growths, glowing spore-pods—breathes together as one interconnected lifeform.

The warm and cool layers of the reduction process mimic bioluminescence and shifting currents, creating an environment that feels submerged yet electric. This world represents my fascination with ecosystems where the landscape is the creature, where every surface pulses, grows, and responds like a single nervous system.

This piece expands my exploration of surreal environments by blending marine biology, extraterrestrial design, and dream logic into a single, immersive biome that feels both welcoming and unsettling.
