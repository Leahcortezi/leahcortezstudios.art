Heaven on Fire

Medium: Mixed media

Dimensions: 8" × 10"

Year: 2021

Project Overview

Heaven on Fire envisions paradise in a state of beautiful collapse—a surreal landscape where divine order gives way to sublime chaos. Through intuitive color relationships and gestural mark-making, this mixed-media composition creates "structured randomness" that mirrors the unpredictable nature of spiritual transformation.

The resulting dreamscape suggests a realm where beauty and terror coexist, where salvation and damnation dissolve into something entirely new. Paradise reimagined—not as eternal perfection, but as constant, magnificent becoming.
