Visual Language of Dream Logic

Medium: Motion Design Year: 2025 Duration: 3:56

Project Overview

Visual Language of Dream Logic is a motion design exploration that translates the non-linear, symbolic nature of dreams into moving image. Through experimental animation techniques and surreal visual metaphors, this work investigates how unconscious narratives construct meaning beyond rational language.

The piece employs shifting perspectives, temporal distortions, and layered imagery to evoke the disorienting yet profound experience of dream states—where logic dissolves and intuitive understanding emerges through symbolic association rather than linear narrative.

Note

This video was created for academic and artistic study. It explores the visual language of dream logic using short excerpts from films, music, and media. This project is non-commercial and all rights remain with the original creators. My contribution focuses on editing, concept development, and visual interpretation.
