Artificial Meadow

Medium: Colored Pencil Year: 2025 Dimensions: 9" x 12"

Artist Statement

Artificial Meadow transforms mundane household objects—sponges, foil, plastic scraps—into a surreal topography where the ordinary becomes magical. A textile scarf's undulating folds evoke rolling hills, while crystalline fragments disrupt the flow with geometric interruptions.

This piece explores the mental act of imaginative reconstruction, becoming a meditation on how perception alters meaning and childhood wonder can be found through artistic misreading.
