Artificial Meadow

Medium: Colored Pencil Year: 2025 Dimensions: 9" x 12"

Project Overview

Artificial Meadow transforms everyday household objects into a fantastical landscape. This piece explores the recontextualization of familiar items to evoke nature, blurring the lines between artificial and organic environments through imaginative reconstruction.

The Challenge

Transform everyday household objects into a fantastical landscape. Explore the recontextualization of familiar items to evoke nature, blurring the lines between artificial and organic environments.

Creative Approach

I arranged mundane materials—sponges, foil, plastic scraps—into a surreal topography, using a textile scarf as the centerpiece. Its undulating folds resembled rolling hills or grassy meadows. Colored pencil added whimsical detailing, referencing the playful surrealism of Dr. Seuss illustrations. Crystalline fragments disrupted the flow with geometric interruptions, suggesting unnatural growths.

Outcome & Exploration

This piece explores the mental act of imaginative reconstruction—how shifting context can make the ordinary magical. By transforming lowly materials into a dreamy terrain, Artificial Meadow becomes a meditation on how perception alters meaning, and how childhood wonder can be found through artistic misreading.
