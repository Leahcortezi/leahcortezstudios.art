Artificial Meadow

Medium: Colored Pencil Year: 2025 Dimensions: 9" x 12"

About The Piece

This drawing reimagines a still life of everyday objects—like a fluffy scarf, crystals, and fabric—into a surreal dreamscape. The scarf transformed into blades of grass, soft and flowing, while the sharp-edged crystals provided contrast, puncturing the scene with tension and texture.

Using colored pencil, I amplified the materials’ qualities, turning soft textures into movement and vibrant color, while the sky swirled into unnatural, glowing patterns. The result is a landscape suspended between the real and the imagined.

Artificial Meadow explores how ordinary objects, when seen differently, can grow into entire worlds.
