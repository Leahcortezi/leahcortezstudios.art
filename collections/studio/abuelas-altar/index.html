Abuela's Altar

Medium: Charcoal Year: 2025 Dimensions: 18" x 24"

About The Piece

Abuela's Altar merges Catholic devotion with ancestral memory through the intimate language of charcoal. Inspired by the ornate complexity of Churrigueresque altars, this still life transforms personal objects into sacred relics—each element a vessel for preserving my grandmother Irma's presence.

The composition weaves together prayer candles, tarot cards, and fragmented keepsakes, creating a visual dialogue between faith and intuition, tradition and personal ritual. Deep contrasts emerge from the charcoal medium, echoing the shadows and light that define memory itself.

This work honors the dual inheritance of fear and faith that Irma carried—her singular desire to reach heaven, and the responsibility she passed down through generations. In assembling this altar, I keep her close while honoring her journey toward the divine.
