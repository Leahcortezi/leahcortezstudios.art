Still Arrangement

Medium: Acrylic and charcoal on paper Year: 2024 Dimensions: 18" x 24"

About The Piece

This atmospheric work captures the liminal space between the conscious and unconscious mind, using layers of translucent color to create depth and mystery. The composition suggests both landscape and mindscape.

Working with a limited palette, the piece explores how subtle shifts in tone and texture can create profound emotional resonance. The charcoal adds gravitas to the ethereal quality of the acrylics.
