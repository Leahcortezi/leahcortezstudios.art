Artificial Meadow

Medium: Oil and charcoal on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 40" x 30"

About The Piece

This powerful work explores the raw energy of natural forces, capturing the moment when elements collide and transform. The bold strokes and dramatic contrasts reflect the volatile beauty of the natural world in motion.

Created through an intuitive process that mirrors the subject matter, the piece emerged from a dialogue between control and spontaneity. The interplay between oil's richness and charcoal's immediacy creates a visual tension that speaks to the duality of creation and destruction.
