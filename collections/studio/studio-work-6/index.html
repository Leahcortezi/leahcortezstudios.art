Textural Meditation

Medium: Mixed media on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 24" x 36"

About The Piece

This work explores the relationship between texture and emotion, using layered materials to create a tactile landscape that invites both visual and visceral response. The piece emerged from a meditation on the way surfaces tell stories.

Through careful building of textures - both literal and implied - the work creates a topography of feeling that speaks to our fundamental human need for touch and connection. Each layer represents a different emotional state or memory.
