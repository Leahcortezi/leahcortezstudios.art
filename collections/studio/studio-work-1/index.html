Studio Work 1

Medium: Mixed media on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 24" × 36" Category: Studio Work

Artist Statement

This piece explores the intersection of memory and identity through layered visual narratives. Using a combination of traditional painting techniques and collage elements, I create a dialogue between past and present experiences.

The composition draws from personal archives and found imagery, creating a palimpsest of meaning that invites viewers to consider their own relationship with memory and transformation. The work challenges conventional boundaries between representation and abstraction.

Through careful attention to color relationships and textural contrasts, the piece evolves into a meditation on the complexity of human experience and the fluid nature of identity in contemporary society.

Process

The work began with a series of gestural marks in charcoal, establishing the underlying energy and movement. Layers of acrylic paint were then applied, with careful attention to color temperature and opacity.

Collage elements were introduced midway through the process, incorporating fragments of personal photographs and text. The final layers involved selective removal and addition of materials, creating areas of transparency and depth.
