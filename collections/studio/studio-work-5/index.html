Color Resonance

Medium: Acrylic on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 30" x 40"

About The Piece

"Color Resonance" explores the emotional and psychological impact of color relationships through bold, gestural brushwork. This piece investigates how colors interact not just visually, but energetically, creating harmonies and tensions that speak to our deeper emotional responses.

The composition emerged from a series of color studies focused on the way certain combinations can evoke memory, emotion, and cultural associations. Through layered applications of paint and dynamic mark-making, the work creates a visual symphony of color that resonates on multiple levels.

Each color choice was informed by both formal considerations and personal associations, creating a work that balances artistic rigor with emotional authenticity. The piece speaks to the power of color as a universal language that transcends cultural boundaries while remaining deeply personal.
