Urban Rhythms

Medium: Mixed media on canvas Year: 2024 Dimensions: 36" x 48"

About The Piece

This dynamic work captures the pulse and energy of city life, translating the visual cacophony of urban environments into a harmonious composition. The layered textures and vibrant colors reflect the complexity of modern existence.

Using found materials and traditional painting techniques, the piece explores the intersection of human creativity and industrial landscape. Each element represents a different aspect of urban experience - from the rush of traffic to quiet moments of reflection.
