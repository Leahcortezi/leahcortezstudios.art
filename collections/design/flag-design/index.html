Flag of Elysia

Medium: Gouache Year: 2025 Size: 17"x11"

A Color Theory Exploration of National Identity & Symbolic Paradise

✶ Challenge

Create a flag for a fictional tropical country that communicates national identity, geographic essence, and aspirational values using two principles of color theory. The flag needed to function at various scales and across merchandise applications, while maintaining conceptual clarity and strong symbolic meaning.

✶ Concept Overview

Elysia is a fictional island nation envisioned as a sun-soaked cultural paradise, rooted in peace, sustainability, and collective harmony. This project reimagined flag design not as a mere identifier, but as a symbolic vessel—a distillation of cultural story and emotional geography.

✶ Design Strategy

Color Theory Concepts Used:

  • Split Complementary Harmony – for visual contrast and tropical vibrancy
  • Simultaneous Contrast – to emphasize emotional warmth and balance between hues

Tripartite Horizontal Structure:
Visually divides the flag into past, present, and future, symbolizing continuity and balance.

Gouache Execution:
Initial renderings were hand-painted to explore organic texture and pigment blending, later refined digitally for scalability.

✶ Symbolic Elements

  • Red Field — Signifies protection, sovereignty, and the strength of the people
  • Green Field — Represents agricultural richness, prosperity, and natural abundance
  • Radiant Central Sunburst — A unifying emblem of hope, unity, and forward momentum, reflecting the nation's solar warmth and island setting
  • Tripartite Layout — Embodies temporal and cultural balance, guiding national vision across generations

✶ Cultural Narrative

Elysia's cultural identity was crafted through research into real-world tropical flags, color psychology, and symbolism. The flag draws from geographic beauty (lush land, warm sun) and aspirational values (unity, growth, peace) to create a cohesive identity. Every element was chosen to reflect life in balance with nature and a vision for a flourishing, sunlit future.
