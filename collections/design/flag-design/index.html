Medium: Digital Design Year: 2025 Type: Vexillological Study

About The Piece

This flag design project explores the art of vexillology—the study of flags—through thoughtful consideration of symbolism, color theory, and cultural representation. Created as part of a graphic design exploration, this piece examines how simple geometric forms and strategic color choices can convey complex meanings and identities.

The design process involved researching historical flag conventions, understanding the psychological impact of different color combinations, and exploring how abstract forms can communicate specific values or concepts. Each element was carefully chosen to balance visual impact with symbolic depth.

Flag design represents one of the purest forms of graphic communication—distilling identity, values, and history into a simple, instantly recognizable symbol. This project challenged me to work within the constraints of the medium while creating something both meaningful and memorable.

The resulting design demonstrates how effective graphic design can transcend decoration to become a powerful tool for storytelling and cultural expression.
