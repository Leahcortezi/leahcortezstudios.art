Flag Design

Medium: Goachue Year: 2025 Size: 17"x11"

About The Piece

This flag design was created as part of a conceptual project exploring visual identity through symbolism, color theory, and minimalist graphic design. Using a limited palette and strong contrast, the design balances simplicity with layered meaning.

The flag represents a fictional country invented for a graphic design exercise. The three horizontal stripes symbolize structure and hierarchy:

  • Red represents power and protection,
  • Green stands for prosperity through agriculture and currency,
  • Yellow symbolizes hope, abundance, and national pride.

The central geometric emblem is a sunburst, expressing growth and optimism at the heart of the nation.

This piece was originally hand-painted during a session at the Goachue workshop, then digitally refined and mocked up using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. It was exhibited at the Mary and Jeff Bell Library at Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi as part of a student design showcase.

By combining conceptual storytelling with graphic design fundamentals, this project reflects how flags—though deceptively simple—can carry powerful narratives of identity and vision.
