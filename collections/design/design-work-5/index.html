Digital Campaign Design

Medium: Digital design, social media Year: 2024 Platform: Multi-platform campaign

About The Project

This comprehensive digital campaign was designed to promote a contemporary art exhibition across multiple platforms. The design system needed to be flexible enough to work across various digital formats while maintaining visual consistency.

The campaign included social media assets, email templates, web banners, and interactive elements. Each piece was optimized for its specific platform while contributing to a cohesive brand narrative.
