Reductive Symbols

Medium: Digital design, brand identity Year: 2024 Client: Cultural Arts Center

About The Project

This comprehensive visual identity system was developed for a contemporary cultural arts center, balancing artistic expression with institutional credibility. The design reflects the organization's mission to bridge traditional and contemporary art forms.

The identity system includes logo design, typography selection, color palette, and application guidelines across digital and print media. Every element was carefully considered to create a cohesive brand experience that resonates with diverse audiences.
