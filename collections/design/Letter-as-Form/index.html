Letter as Form – Abstract Grid Composition

Medium: Typography, experimental design Year: 2025

Concept

This project reimagines typographic forms as visual elements independent of linguistic meaning.

Design Approach

Using a strict 3x3 grid, I manipulated bold serif glyphs by rotating, cropping, and scaling them to form interlocking patterns. The play between positive and negative space became essential for rhythm and contrast.

Result

The final composition challenges typographic conventions and showcases letterforms as expressive building blocks with sculptural potential.
