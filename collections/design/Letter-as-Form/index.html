Letter as Form

Medium: Typography, experimental design Year: 2024 Type: Typography exploration

About The Project

Letter as Form is an experimental typography project that explores the intersection of written language and visual form. This project examines how letterforms can transcend their traditional role as carriers of meaning to become powerful visual elements in their own right.

The design approach focuses on the sculptural qualities of typography, treating each letter as both a functional element and an artistic form. Through careful manipulation of scale, spacing, and composition, the project reveals the inherent beauty and expressive potential of letterforms.

This exploration bridges the gap between typography and fine art, demonstrating how traditional design elements can be reimagined to create new forms of visual communication and artistic expression.
