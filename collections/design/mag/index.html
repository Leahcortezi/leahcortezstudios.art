Editorial Spread

Medium: Editorial Spread, Digital Print Year: 2025 Dimensions: 11 x 17 in

Project Overview

This editorial spread was created for a project where I selected an article and designed a magazine layout around it. I chose "Alien Invasion or Sleep Paralysis?" because the topic fascinated me and offered a chance to create visually chaotic, dreamy compositions.

Inspired by David Carson, I used cropped images of an alien face and a shadowy figure to emphasize the confusion between sleep paralysis and alien abduction—two experiences that are often mixed up in people's minds. The design aims to visualize that confusion, blending surreal imagery and layered text to create a sense of uncertainty and dreamlike tension.
