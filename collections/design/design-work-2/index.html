Editorial Spreads

Voces Nuevas Magazine

Medium: Editorial design, print layout

Project Type: Magazine design and layout

Year: 2024

Category: Design Work

Pages: 48-page quarterly publication

Project Overview

Voces Nuevas is a quarterly magazine dedicated to showcasing contemporary Chicana artists and their contributions to the cultural landscape. The design balances contemporary typography with cultural elements that honor traditional aesthetics.

The layout system was developed to accommodate both Spanish and English text, ensuring accessibility while maintaining visual consistency. Special attention was paid to image presentation, allowing artwork to be the primary focus while supporting it with thoughtful typography.

The magazine serves as a platform for emerging and established artists, providing them with a beautifully designed space to share their stories and showcase their work to a broader audience.

Design Approach

The design process began with extensive research into the featured artists and their work, ensuring that the layout would complement rather than compete with the artwork. A flexible grid system was developed to accommodate various image sizes and orientations.

Typography choices reflect the bilingual nature of the publication, with careful consideration given to line length, hierarchy, and readability in both languages. Color palettes were developed to work harmoniously with the featured artwork.

Collaboration with artists was essential throughout the process, ensuring their vision was properly represented while maintaining overall design cohesion.

Technical Details

Format: 8.5" × 11" perfect bound

Typography: Custom font pairing with sans-serif headers and serif body text

Color: Full-color with spot color accents

Paper: Matte finish for optimal artwork reproduction

Print Run: 5,000 copies quarterly
