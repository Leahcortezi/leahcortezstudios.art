Animated Logotype

Threads

Static and Animated Logotype

Typography II 2026

Concept

This project develops a visual identity system for Threads, a contemporary clothing retail brand targeting young, fashion-conscious consumers and local trendsetters. The concept centers on circulation—of fabric, of trends, and of people—positioning clothing as a dynamic system rather than a static product. The logotype and motion system reflect the fluid exchange between individual expression and collective culture.

Design Approach

The logotype is constructed with bold, condensed letterforms that emphasize verticality and movement. Custom typographic manipulation subtly interlocks characters, referencing the physical interweaving of threads within fabric. The static mark maintains clarity and strength, while the animated version introduces rotational and looping motion to visually reinforce the idea of circulation and continuity.

A limited black-and-white base palette ensures versatility across retail applications, while selective accent colors add energy within digital contexts. The system is designed to function cohesively across storefront signage, social media, packaging, and motion graphics—maintaining legibility while adapting to diverse environments.

Result

The final identity system balances bold urban presence with conceptual depth. By translating the idea of "threads" into both structural typography and motion, the brand communicates movement, community, and trend evolution. The logo functions effectively across static and animated formats, establishing a cohesive and adaptable visual language for the retail space.
