Themed Playing Card Design

Medium: Digital design Year: 2024

About The Piece

This conceptual playing card deck reimagines the classic King cards to represent four major scientific fields—Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Technology. Each King is illustrated with historical figures and symbolic tools of their discipline, from microscopes and DNA strands to gears and circuit motifs.

Inspired by Jay Fletcher’s geometric visual language, the design blends timeless academic themes with modern minimalism. Muted tones, textured elements, and symmetrical backs unify the set as both a tactile and educational tribute to discovery, legacy, and play.
