Themed Playing Card Design

Medium: Digital design Year: 2024 Category: Graphic Design

About The Piece

“Scientific Revolution” is a themed playing card set that reimagines the traditional King face card format to honor major scientific disciplines and their historical figures. Each card in the deck represents a field—Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Technology—featuring illustrated scientists and embedded symbolism tied to their area of study.

My original concept began with a bold sci-fi aesthetic, but evolved into a more refined academic design. Muted tones, geometric patterns, and a clean white background were chosen to reflect the legacy and seriousness of scholarly achievement while still remaining playful and engaging.

Inspired by Jay Fletcher’s geometric and symbolic illustration style, I created simplified portraits and designed scepters, crowns, and backgrounds out of meaningful scientific tools—microscopes, gears, lightbulbs, DNA strands. Each King’s crown emerges from a patterned sash that reflects their specific discipline.

The card backs feature a unified symmetrical motif representing the pursuit of knowledge, adding cohesion and visual rhythm to the deck. Subtle texture and grain were applied in Photoshop to give the cards a tactile, worn quality—bridging timeless academia with modern clarity.

“Scientific Revolution” is both a tribute to discovery and a graphic system designed to educate, honor, and play.
