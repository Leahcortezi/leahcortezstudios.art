Scientific Revolution – Themed Playing Card Deck

Medium: Digital design Year: 2024

Concept

This playing card deck blends science history with traditional card formats, using historical figures as Kings of their fields—Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Technology.

Design Approach

Each card features a custom illustration embedded with discipline-specific icons and color palettes. The back design unifies the deck with a symmetrical emblem, reinforcing the academic tone.

Result

A modernized educational artifact that celebrates intellectual legacy through playful design, honoring historical accuracy while maintaining clarity and visual appeal.
