How to Be a Balloon Twister – Book Cover Design

Medium: Book cover design, typography Year: 2024

Concept

This design reinvents the classic "how-to" manual with bold geometry, a 3x3 grid, and a nod to retro instructional design.

Design Approach

Inflated, translucent geometric shapes mimic balloon forms. Directional arrows and cropped typography guide the viewer's eye, while the black-and-white palette creates contrast and legibility.

Sketches

Result

A dynamic, instructional cover that balances playful tone with modern composition—making abstract structure feel light and accessible.
