Medium: Digital design, print applications Year: 2024

About The Piece

This typographic book cover reimagines a profession through the lens of design systems. Inspired by the playful yet methodical nature of balloon twisting, the layout uses bold geometry, directional arrows, and abstracted forms to reflect movement, instruction, and tension.

The cover was built within a strict 3x3 grid, emphasizing control while allowing for expressive curves that echo inflated shapes. Overlapping elements, fragmented text, and arrows guide the viewer’s eye—mimicking the logic of DIY manuals and step-by-step creation.

How to Be a Balloon Twister is an exercise in visual rhythm, coded structure, and design as translation—where typography becomes the instruction.
