Apology – Reductive Symbol Design

Medium: Typography, experimental design Year: 2024

Concept

Using the letter "n", this reductive design transforms type into a figure expressing emotional sincerity and vulnerability.

Design Approach

The letterform is restructured to resemble a slouched figure, referencing nonverbal apology. Each curve is derived from the original typeface to maintain continuity.

Sketches

Result

A symbol that merges abstract form with emotional clarity, showing how typography can embody human expression.
