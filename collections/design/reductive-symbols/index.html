Reductive Symbols

Medium: Print design, typography Year: 2024 Pages: 128 pages

About The Project

This exhibition catalog was designed for a major retrospective, requiring careful consideration of typography, image placement, and paper selection to honor both the artworks and the reader's experience. The design serves as a lasting document of the exhibition.

The layout balances white space with rich imagery, allowing each artwork to breathe while maintaining visual cohesion throughout the publication. Special attention was paid to color accuracy and print quality to ensure faithful reproduction of the original works.
