About Me

Academic Journey

I'm Leah Cortez, a first-year Chicana graphic design student at Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi, pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Studio Art. I'm passionate about developing my skills in visual communication and building a career in design.

Design Philosophy

I believe design should be both purposeful and meaningful. My approach centers on understanding the story behind each project—who it serves, what it communicates, and how it can create authentic connections. I'm learning to balance aesthetic beauty with functional clarity, ensuring every design choice serves the greater narrative.

What Sets Me Apart

I bring a distinctive creative voice that seamlessly integrates cultural storytelling with contemporary design innovation. My work is distinguished by its symbolic depth to create emotionally resonant communications. I approach typography as an expressive medium, transforming functional elements into powerful narrative tools that connect authentically with diverse audiences while maintaining sophisticated aesthetic appeal.

Available for Projects

As I continue my design education, I'm actively seeking graphic design opportunities, collaborations, and client projects. I'm excited to work with individuals and organizations who value thoughtful, culturally-informed design solutions.
