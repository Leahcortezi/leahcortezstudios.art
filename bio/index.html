About Me

Professional Background

I'm Leah Cortez, a Chicana graphic designer and studio artist based in Corpus Christi, Texas. I hold a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Studio Art from Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi. My work bridges emotional storytelling and visual strategy, blending cultural identity, symbolism, and experimentation across mediums.

Design Expertise

My design practice spans brand identity, publication design, digital graphics, and expressive typography. I approach every project with a balance of strategic clarity and creative depth—building visual systems that carry meaning. I see design as a method of storytelling, where structure and emotion meet to communicate with purpose.

Studio Art Practice

Rooted in symbolic and experimental image-making, my studio work explores themes of memory, femininity, and inherited trauma. I work in mixed media, sculpture, and digital collage, with plans to further develop my voice through printmaking. In sculpture, I'm especially drawn to bending and distorting surfaces—pushing form into surreal, uncanny structures that serve as vessels for emotion and cultural reflection.

Creative Approach

I blend design methodology with artistic intuition—creating concept-driven work that reflects both visual intelligence and emotional sensitivity. My Chicana heritage plays a central role in how I build narratives, using layered materials and symbolic form to explore identity and belonging. Whether working independently or collaboratively, I approach each project with intention, research, and heart.

Collaboration & Direction

I value collaboration as a site for trust, meaning, and transformation. I work closely with clients, artists, and creative teams to bring abstract ideas to life—translating stories, values, and visions into cohesive visual outcomes. I'm currently open to commissions, exhibitions, and creative partnerships that explore identity, ritual, and emotional symbolism through design and art.

My Creative Process

1. Discovery & Connection

I begin every project by deeply understanding your story, values, and goals. This includes exploring cultural context, target audience, and the emotional core of what you want to communicate.

2. Research & Inspiration

I gather visual references, cultural symbols, and conceptual frameworks that align with your vision. This research phase ensures authenticity and meaningful representation.

3. Concept Development

Using storytelling as my foundation, I develop multiple creative directions that balance artistic expression with strategic communication goals.

4. Collaborative Refinement

Through iterative feedback sessions, we refine the design together, ensuring it captures both your vision and resonates with your audience.

5. Final Delivery

I provide polished, professional files ready for implementation, along with guidance on how to maintain consistency across all applications.
