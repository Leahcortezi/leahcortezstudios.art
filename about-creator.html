About the Creator

Authentic art rooted in Hispanic culture and community

Hispana Artist from Corpus Christi

I'm a graphic design student creating art that honors Hispanic traditions while pushing creative boundaries. My work bridges the sacred symbols of our culture—like the sacred heart—with contemporary design, making space for authentic Hispanic voices in modern art.

Growing up in South Texas, I've witnessed the beauty of our community and the need for genuine representation. Each piece I create carries this mission: to preserve our cultural heritage while making it accessible to new generations.

My Inspo and Roots

Growing up in Corpus Christi, I was surrounded by rich Hispanic culture—worshipping Virgen de Guadalupe, celebrating Selena's legacy, and attending art walks every Friday where I saw beautiful local Hispanic art. My mother practiced bruja for protection, which taught me that art and spirituality are deeply connected. But when I looked at mainstream media, none of our culture was represented.

As a teen, I found myself in two subcultures that would shape my artistic voice: the raw energy of our local music scene and the authentic spirit of skater culture. These experiences taught me that art should be real, unfiltered, and true to your community.

Now in college, I'm exploring new mediums while getting my name out there, but my roots remain the foundation of everything I create.
