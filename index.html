About the Artist

I'm Leah Cortez, a Chicana artist and designer from Corpus Christi, South Texas. I make work about identity, religion, and the uncomfortable across graphic design, printmaking, illustration, and mixed media. This site is my archive.

The Unholy Gaze

Aquatint Etching • 2025

Unholy Gaze is about how religion often frames women as unholy—something to be watched, judged, and controlled. Through aquatint textures and stark contrast, the work emphasizes vulnerability and power imbalance, confronting the way religious structures reduce women to symbols of virtue or corruption.
