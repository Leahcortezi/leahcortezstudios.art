About the Artist

Leah Cortez is a Chicana artist and designer from Corpus Christi, Texas, currently studying Graphic Design with a minor in Studio Art at Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi. Her work explores identity, symbolism, and storytelling across both traditional and digital media.
