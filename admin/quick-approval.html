🎨 Quick Approval Tool

Streamlined artwork approval system - copy from emails and approve with one click

📧 How to Use This Tool

  1. Receive submission email from FormSubmit with artwork details
  2. Copy information from the email into the form below
  3. Save the image attachment from the email to your computer
  4. Upload the image and fill out all required fields
  5. Click "Approve & Add" to instantly publish to the community showcase

Artwork Approval Form

Save the image attachment from the submission email first

⚡ How This Works

FormSubmit + Webhook: When you submit this form, it triggers two actions:

1. Email confirmation sent to you with approval details

2. Webhook triggers your API at /api/add-artwork.php

3. Artwork automatically appears in the community showcase

No Zapier needed - this uses FormSubmit's built-in webhook feature!
